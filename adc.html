Scope of the project

I’ve designed a promo page for a large online conference in the design industry. This year professional designers, architects, studio owners, and people from the industry come together to take stock of the year and share the secrets gained over the season. My task was to redesign a visual concept of the brand to attract a new target audience and improve the marketing side.
